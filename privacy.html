SPLASH

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Splash ("the app") is an alarm app that verifies you are awake using a wet-face selfie. This policy explains what data the app handles, how, and why. The short version: your selfies are never stored, we don't sell data, and we collect the minimum needed to run the app.

1. Who we are

Splash is developed by Leo López Cañadas, an independent developer based in Spain. Contact: leolopezcanadas@gmail.com.

2. Selfie photos (camera)

Your wake-up selfies are processed ephemerally and never stored.

When an alarm rings, Splash asks you to take a selfie with a wet face to prove you're awake. That photo is:

  • Sent over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) to our verification service, which uses an AI model to answer one question: does this photo show a wet face?
  • Processed in memory only, never written to storage, never used to train AI models, and discarded immediately after the check.
  • Never used to identify you, and never shared with anyone beyond the AI processing described above.

Our verification runs on Supabase (hosting) and Anthropic (AI analysis), acting as processors under our instructions.

3. Purchases and subscriptions

Subscriptions are handled by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, our subscription infrastructure provider. RevenueCat receives anonymous purchase information (transaction details and a random app-specific identifier) so the app knows whether your subscription is active. We never see or store your payment details — Apple handles all payment processing.

4. Data stored on your device

Your alarms, wake-up history, and streaks are stored locally on your device only. We do not run user accounts and we cannot access this data.

5. What we do NOT do

  • No ads, no trackers, no analytics SDKs that profile you.
  • No selling or renting of any data.
  • No storing of photos, ever.
  • No collection of contacts, location, or health data.

6. Legal basis (GDPR)

For users in the EU/EEA: we process your selfie under Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — it is strictly necessary to provide the service you request (wake-up verification). Ephemeral processing means no personal data is retained after each verification.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR and similar laws you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Because selfies are not stored and app data lives only on your device, most requests can be satisfied by deleting the app. For anything else, email us and we'll respond within 30 days.

8. Children

Splash is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from children under 13.

9. Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated in the app.
