Frequently asked questions

The alarm didn't stop after my selfie

Verification needs an internet connection. If it fails repeatedly, the app lets you dismiss honestly (your streak just won't count). Check your connection and camera permission in Settings → Splash.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Splash Pro → Cancel. If you cancel during the free trial, you won't be charged.

How do I restore my purchase on a new phone?

Open the paywall and tap "Restore Purchases" while signed in with the same Apple Account.

Are my selfies stored anywhere?

No. Photos are verified in memory and deleted immediately. See our Privacy Policy.
